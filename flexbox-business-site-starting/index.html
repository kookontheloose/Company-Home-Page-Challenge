Santosha Lānaʻi, A Four Seasons Resort

Beneath the overhanging branches of island pines and gently swaying palms, discover Ātman Santosha, A Four Seasons Resort.

Would you like to get away for a few days and be rejuvenated? Are you looking for a healthy vacation or a weekend spa retreat? Our 3 day wellness retreat will leave you feeling lighter, cleaner, rested, inspired and relaxed. Enjoy your time in the heart of beautiful historic, Lānaʻi island, eating organic raw food, drinking freshly pressed juices, attending yoga, hiking ancient trails and snorkeling in pristine warm waters. In the evenings you can unwind while watching incredible sunsets from the chlorine-free pool.

Ātman Santosha means 'Soul Contentment' - this is what we strive to provide for you. Our mission is to cultivate peace of mind. We strive to nourish local and global communities by promoting sustainability, abundance, and wellness while celebrating diversity with a respect for culture and sacred lands.
